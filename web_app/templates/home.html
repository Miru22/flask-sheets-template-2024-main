Simple Justice

This website purpose is to provide a repository of how judges rule. It is to provide emperical data.

Let's perform a simple thought experiment. If you were to look up a Congressman, Senator, or even a city counsilman how the individual voted on issues it would not much digging besides a few Google searches to find the information.

The problem with judges however, is that their decisions impact children and families. Getting these decisions wrong has lifelong impacts.

How to use this site.

First: Upload Custody Orders

Specifically we are looking for family custody case orders. This will determine aspects of a parents involvements like medical decision-making, time spent with each parents.

Second: Data Processing

We will try to process the data within a few days.

Third: Evaluate

Our intent is to provide a suite of data evaluation tools with references to the soruces, so you can evaluate the judges in your community

Fourth: Activate!

Use this data to inform yourself or take action to change the judiciary. Ultimately our objective is to get better rulings to support childrens safety and development.

Explore: If you want to see how judges rule, check out our analytics page.This is a great spot to example how judges are ruling.
